I'm actively leading a talented engineer team, where we specialize in Backend, DevOps, Web, iOS, and Android development. We focus on delivering scalable, high-performance solutions for businesses worldwide. Whether it’s infrastructure setup, API design, mobile and web development, or full product lifecycle management, we ensure high-quality, secure, and efficient software development.

I am passionate about technical leadership, AI-driven applications, decentralized networking solutions, and community-driven platforms. I actively work on projects that push the boundaries of technology while ensuring practical, business-focused results.

My products have reached a global audience, with over 16 million downloads on the App Store and Google Play. I also have strong experience in outsourcing solutions, helping international clients bring their ideas to life with custom-built software tailored to their needs.

Beyond work, I enjoy building mobile and macOS apps in my free time. To stay active, I train in Japanese sword art and culture, which helps me maintain both physical and mental discipline. When I’m not coding or training, you’ll likely find me playing PlayStation games, as I occasionally like to unwind with gaming.

If you’d like to discuss a project or collaboration, feel free to contact me at longtrieuquang@gmail.com.
